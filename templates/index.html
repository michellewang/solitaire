Goal of the game:
Fill up the Foundations with cards from the same suit, ranked from Ace to King.

How to Play:
Click on a card to select it. To unselect it, click on it again. Click on another card to move the selected card.

Allowed moves:
Flip 1 or 3 cards from the Stock to the Waste (you can change this in Settings).
Move one card from the Waste to a Foundation or to the Tableau.
Move card(s) from one Tableau pile to another.
Move card from the Tableau to a Foundation.

What's What:
The Stock: The pile of facedown cards in the upper left corner.
The Waste: The faceup pile next to the Stock in the upper left corner.
The Foundations: The four piles in the upper right corner.
The Tableau: The seven piles that make up the main table.
